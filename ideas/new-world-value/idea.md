## Core Thesis
The old metrics of success (GDP, stock price, home ownership) are failing individuals. A new value system — rooted in security, meaning, access, intelligence, and trust — is emerging. This channel maps it, names it, and teaches how to live inside it.

## Why Now?
March 2026 is the inflection point: Hormuz crisis, US-Iran war, record uncertainty index, K/E-shaped economies, AI layoffs accelerating, Gen Z disillusionomics peaking.
