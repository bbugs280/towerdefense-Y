## 6. Technical Feasibility Assessment

### Current Stack (Your Prototype)

| Component | Technology | Status | Risk |
|-----------|------------|--------|------|
| **Mobile App** | Flutter 3.11 | ✅ Working | Low |
| **Backend** | FastAPI + WebSocket | ✅ Working | Low |
| **Vision AI** | Qwen-VL-Max (DashScope) | ✅ Working | Low (API dependency) |
| **TTS** | CosyVoice + flutter_tts | ✅ Working | Low |
| **Languages** | EN/ZH/YUE/JA | ✅ Working | Low |
| **Deployment** | Docker + Cloudflare Tunnel | ✅ Working | Low |

### Technical Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Severity | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| **DashScope API downtime** | Medium | Low | Fallback to native TTS; cache recent responses |
| **WebSocket disconnection (mobile networks)** | Medium | Medium | Auto-reconnect logic; queue frames locally |
| **Battery drain (continuous camera)** | High | High | Adaptive frame rate (5s → 15s when idle); background optimization |
| **CosyVoice latency (>2s)** | Medium | Medium | Show "thinking…" animation; stream tokens progressively |
| **On-device ML (future)** | Medium | N/A | Plan TFLite migration; test MediaPipe Pose first |
| **Scale (10K+ concurrent users)** | Low | Low | Horizontal scaling (Kubernetes); CDN for audio files |

### Feasibility Score: **9/10**

Prototype proves core functionality works. Remaining work is optimization + polish, not R&D.

## 7. Key Risks

### Risk Matrix

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|--------|------------|------------|-------|
| **Regulatory: Misleading claims ("mind reading")** | High | Medium | Clear disclaimers; position as "entertainment, not translation" | Legal |
| **Privacy: Camera frames leave device** | High | High | Add "local-only mode"; document data retention; PDPO compliance | Engineering |
| **Competitive: Major player copies (Google, Amazon)** | Medium | Low | Speed to market; Cantonese moat; community building | Strategy |
| **Technical: API cost overrun at scale** | Medium | Medium | Usage metering; tiered pricing; optimize frame sampling | Engineering |
| **Market: Novelty wears off (low retention)** | High | Medium | Feedback loop; social sharing; personality customization | Product |
| **Ethical: Vet/behaviorist pushback** | Low | Low | Advisory board; "not veterinary advice" disclaimers | Legal |

### Risk Summary
- **Critical (must address before launch):** Regulatory positioning, Privacy architecture
- **Important (address in first 90 days):** Retention strategy, API cost optimization
- **Monitor (watch, no immediate action):** Competitive copying, ethical concerns

## Appendix C: Disclaimer Copy (App Store + In-App)

### Short Version (App Store Description)
> *Paw Mind is for entertainment purposes only. Dogs do not have human-like inner monologues. Paw Mind interprets behavior using AI and generates playful "thoughts" based on observed actions. Not a veterinary or behavioral diagnostic tool.*

### Long Version (In-App Onboarding)
> **What Paw Mind Does:**  
> Uses AI to analyze your dog's body language, facial expressions, and vocalizations — then generates playful, personalized "thoughts" in a voice you choose.
>
> **What Paw Mind Does NOT Do:**  
> - Read your dog's actual thoughts (dogs don't think in words!)  
> - Replace veterinary advice or professional behavior consultation  
> - Guarantee accuracy (it's for fun, not science)
>
> **Privacy:**  
> - Camera frames are processed server-side (DashScope API)  
> - You can enable "Local-Only Mode" to disable server calls  
> - We do not store or share your dog's images without consent  
>
> By using Paw Mind, you acknowledge this is an entertainment product.
