# 🐾 PawMind — Project Home

*Continuous camera + character voice dog mind interface*

## Vision
To deepen the human-dog bond through joyful, private, and expressive AI — starting in Hong Kong.

## Non-Negotiables
- ✅ **On-device only**: No cloud inference, no video/audio upload — all processing happens on the phone.
- ✅ **HK-first**: Cantonese voice support, HK pet regulations compliance, local user testing baked in.
- ✅ **Warmth over accuracy**: Voice must feel emotionally resonant — not robotic or clinical.
- ✅ **Prompt-driven personality**: Users shape *how* their dog speaks — not just *what* they say.
